# 05: Personal Branding Prompts

Build authority, attract opportunities, and become the go-to expert in your niche. These prompts help you craft your brand story, positioning, and content strategy so people come to you instead of you chasing them.

## Prompt 1: Personal Brand Statement

### Prompt

```
Help me craft a personal brand statement.

About me:
- Name: [YOUR NAME]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- What I do: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF WORK]
- Who I help: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- My unique angle: [DIFFERENTIATOR]
- My biggest achievement: [ACHIEVEMENT]
- How I want people to describe me: [3 ADJECTIVES]

Create:
1. **One-liner** (under 15 words): "I help [audience] [achieve result] through [method]"
2. **Elevator pitch** (30 seconds / 75 words): Hook → credibility → value → CTA
3. **Full brand story** (250 words): Origin story → turning point → mission → vision
4. **Tagline** (5 variations): Memorable, differentiating, repeatable
5. **Brand pillars** (3-4): The core themes you want to be known for, with a 1-sentence description of each

Rules:
- Must feel authentic, not corporate
- Should attract your ideal audience and repel the wrong one
- Specific > generic ("I help SaaS founders reduce churn by 30%" beats "I help businesses grow")
```

## Prompt 2: Thought Leadership Content Plan

### Prompt

```
Create a 30-day thought leadership content plan for [YOUR NAME] on [PLATFORM].

Context:
- Expertise: [WHAT YOU WANT TO BE KNOWN FOR]
- Target audience: [WHO YOU'RE BUILDING AUTHORITY WITH]
- Current following: [APPROXIMATE SIZE]
- Content goal: [GROW AUDIENCE / ATTRACT CLIENTS / LAND SPEAKING GIGS / GET HIRED]
- Hot takes I have: [2-3 OPINIONS THAT MIGHT BE CONTROVERSIAL]

Plan structure:
- Week 1 (Days 1-7): **Establish credibility** — Share experience, results, and insights
- Week 2 (Days 8-14): **Add value** — Teach frameworks, share processes, give away knowledge
- Week 3 (Days 15-21): **Spark conversation** — Hot takes, questions, debates
- Week 4 (Days 22-30): **Social proof + CTA** — Testimonials, case studies, offers

For each day:
1. Content type (post, thread, video, story, carousel)
2. Topic
3. Hook (first line)
4. Key message
5. CTA

Mix: 40% educational, 30% personal story, 20% opinion, 10% promotional.
```

## Prompt 3: LinkedIn Profile Optimization

### Prompt

```
Optimize my LinkedIn profile for [CAREER GOAL] in [INDUSTRY].

Current info:
- Name: [YOUR NAME]
- Current role: [TITLE AT COMPANY]
- Experience summary: [2-3 SENTENCES]
- Key achievements: [LIST 3-5]
- Skills: [TOP 5 SKILLS]
- Education: [RELEVANT EDUCATION]
- What I want LinkedIn to do for me: [ATTRACT CLIENTS / RECRUIT OFFERS / BUILD NETWORK / THOUGHT LEADERSHIP]

Rewrite:
1. **Headline** (120 chars): Not just your job title — value proposition + keywords
2. **About section** (2,000 chars max):
   - Hook (first 2 lines — must earn the "see more" click)
   - What I do and who I help (paragraph 1)
   - My approach/philosophy (paragraph 2)
   - Proof — results and achievements (paragraph 3)
   - CTA — how to work with me or connect (paragraph 4)
   - Contact info and link
3. **Featured section**: 3 items to showcase (with descriptions)
4. **Experience bullets**: Rewrite top 2 roles as achievement-focused (not responsibility-focused)
5. **Skills order**: Prioritize the 5 most relevant for my goal
6. **Recommendations**: Draft a request message I can send to 3 colleagues

SEO: Include keywords that [TARGET AUDIENCE] would search for when looking for someone like me.
```

## Prompt 4: Speaking / Podcast Pitch

### Prompt

```
Write pitch emails to get me on [podcasts / speaking stages / panels] about [TOPIC].

About me:
- Name: [YOUR NAME]
- Expertise: [WHAT I SPEAK ABOUT]
- Audience I reach: [MY PLATFORM/FOLLOWING]
- Past speaking/podcast experience: [LIST ANY]
- Unique angle: [WHY MY PERSPECTIVE IS DIFFERENT]
- Key achievement: [MOST IMPRESSIVE RESULT]

Create:
1. **Cold pitch email** (under 150 words):
   - Subject line (3 variations)
   - Why I'd be valuable for THEIR audience (not about me — about them)
   - 3 episode/talk topics I could cover
   - Social proof (1-2 sentences)
   - Soft CTA
2. **Speaker one-sheet** content:
   - Bio (50 words, third person)
   - Bio (150 words, third person)
   - 5 talk topics with titles and 1-sentence descriptions
   - Headshot description/requirements
   - Social links and contact
3. **Follow-up email** (if no response after 7 days — under 50 words)

Tone: Confident but not arrogant. Show value to their audience, not your ego.
```

## Prompt 5: Brand Voice Guide

### Prompt

```
Create a brand voice guide for my personal brand.

About me:
- Name: [YOUR NAME]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- Personality: [3-5 PERSONALITY TRAITS]
- Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- Brands I admire (voice-wise): [2-3 EXAMPLES — e.g., "Sahil Lavingia's transparency + James Clear's clarity"]
- Words I'd use: [5 WORDS THAT FEEL LIKE ME]
- Words I'd never use: [5 WORDS THAT DON'T FIT]

Create:
1. **Voice characteristics** (4 pillars): Each with:
   - Description (what it sounds like)
   - Do this (examples of good execution)
   - Don't do this (examples of what to avoid)
2. **Tone spectrum**: How the voice adapts across contexts:
   - Social media (most casual)
   - Blog/newsletter (medium)
   - Professional bio / proposals (most formal)
3. **Vocabulary guide**:
   - 10 power words to use frequently
   - 10 words/phrases to avoid
   - Jargon policy (use it? Explain it? Avoid it?)
4. **Example rewrites**: Take 3 generic sentences and rewrite them in my voice
5. **The "sounds like me" test**: 5 questions to ask before publishing anything

This guide should make it possible for anyone (or any AI) to write in my voice.
```

## Prompt 6: Portfolio / Case Study Presentation

### Prompt

```
Help me present my work as a portfolio or case study for [CAREER GOAL].

Project details:
- Project name: [NAME]
- Client/company: [WHO IT WAS FOR]
- My role: [WHAT I DID]
- Challenge: [THE PROBLEM]
- Solution: [WHAT I BUILT/DID]
- Results: [MEASURABLE OUTCOMES]
- Timeline: [HOW LONG]
- Tools used: [TECHNOLOGIES/METHODS]

Create:
1. **Portfolio headline**: Result-focused (e.g., "Increased revenue 40% through...")
2. **The Story** (STAR format, 300 words):
   - Situation: Context and stakes
   - Task: What was needed
   - Action: What I specifically did (not the team — me)
   - Result: Metrics and impact
3. **Visual suggestions**: What screenshots, charts, or mockups to include
4. **Key learnings**: 2-3 insights from this project
5. **Testimonial prompt**: Question to ask the client to get a great quote

Format for:
- Website portfolio page
- LinkedIn featured post
- Slide deck (5 slides)
```

## Prompt 7: Networking Email Templates

### Prompt

```
Write networking email templates for [YOUR NAME] in [INDUSTRY].

Context:
- My role: [CURRENT ROLE]
- What I'm looking for: [CAREER GOAL — clients, mentor, job, partnerships, etc.]
- My value add: [WHAT I CAN OFFER THEM]

Create templates for:
1. **Cold intro to someone you admire**: Specific compliment + value-first ask
2. **Warm intro request**: Ask a mutual connection to introduce you
3. **After meeting at an event**: Follow up within 24 hours
4. **Reconnecting with an old contact**: Natural re-engagement after long silence
5. **Offering help**: Provide value before asking for anything
6. **Requesting a coffee chat / virtual meeting**: Specific agenda, easy to say yes
7. **Thank you after a meeting**: Recap + next steps

Rules for all:
- Under 100 words each
- Always lead with value (what's in it for them)
- Include a specific, easy CTA
- No "I'd love to pick your brain" (everyone hates that)
- Personalization hooks: What to research about them before writing
```

## Prompt 8: Online Course / Workshop Outline

### Prompt

```
Create an outline for an online [course / workshop] teaching [TOPIC].

Context:
- Instructor: [YOUR NAME]
- Topic: [WHAT YOU'RE TEACHING]
- Target student: [WHO IS THIS FOR AND THEIR CURRENT LEVEL]
- Format: [self-paced video / live cohort / workshop / bootcamp]
- Duration: [TOTAL LENGTH]
- Price point: [PRICE]
- End result: [WHAT THEY'LL BE ABLE TO DO AFTER]

Outline:
1. **Course title** (3 variations — benefit-focused)
2. **Course description** (200 words — for sales page)
3. **Learning outcomes**: 5 specific, measurable skills they'll gain
4. **Module breakdown** (5-8 modules):
   - Module title
   - Learning objective
   - Lesson topics (3-5 per module)
   - Exercise/assignment
   - Estimated time to complete
5. **Bonus materials**: What extras to include (templates, checklists, community access)
6. **Assessment**: How students prove they've learned (project, quiz, peer review)
7. **Sales page elements**: Testimonial prompts, FAQ, guarantee, pricing anchoring

The transformation should be clear: "Go from [current state] to [desired state] in [timeframe]."
```

### Prompt

```
Create professional bios and email signature for [YOUR NAME].

Context:
- Name: [YOUR NAME]

- Company: [COMPANY NAME]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- Key achievement: [MOST IMPRESSIVE RESULT/CREDENTIAL]
- Website: [URL]
- LinkedIn: [URL]
- Email: [EMAIL]
- Phone: [PHONE — optional]

Create:
1. **25-word bio**: For Twitter, conference programs, brief intros
2. **75-word bio**: For podcast intros, article bylines, webinar descriptions
3. **200-word bio**: For speaking pages, About pages, press kits
4. **Third-person versions** of all three (for when someone else introduces you)
5. **Email signature**:
   - Clean, professional format (no images that break in email)
   - Name, title, company
   - One-liner value proposition
   - 2 links max (website + LinkedIn or calendar)
   - Optional: recent content or award

Rules:
- Lead with what you DO, not where you went to school
- Include one specific metric or achievement
- End with personality (a quirk or interest that makes you human)
```

## Prompt 10: Personal Brand Audit

### Prompt

```
Conduct a personal brand audit for [YOUR NAME].

Current presence:
- LinkedIn: [URL or description of current state]
- Twitter/X: [URL or description]
- Website/portfolio: [URL or description]
- Other platforms: [LIST]
- Last 10 pieces of content I published: [BRIEF LIST]
- How people currently find me: [REFERRAL / SEARCH / SOCIAL / EVENTS]
- How I want to be perceived: [3 ADJECTIVES]

Audit:
1. **Consistency check**: Is my message the same across all platforms?
2. **First impression**: What would a stranger conclude about me in 30 seconds?
3. **SEO**: What shows up when someone Googles my name? What SHOULD show up?
4. **Content alignment**: Does my content support my goals or just get likes?
5. **Visual identity**: Is there a cohesive look/feel across platforms?
6. **Gaps**: What's missing that my target audience expects to see?
7. **Competitive positioning**: How do I compare to 3 peers in my space?

Provide:
- Score (1-10) for each area
- Top 3 quick wins (can fix this week)
- Top 3 strategic priorities (next 90 days)
- Specific action items for each platform
```

## Pro Tips

- **Consistency beats virality**: Posting 3x/week for a year beats going viral once
- **Share the journey, not just the destination**: People connect with process, struggles, and growth — not just wins
- **Be known for ONE thing first**: It's tempting to be "the marketing + design + strategy + AI person." Pick one. Dominate it. Expand later.
- **Your brand is what people say when you're not in the room**: Build it intentionally or the market will build it for you
- **Document, don't create**: The easiest content comes from documenting what you're already doing
