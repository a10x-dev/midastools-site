# Midas Tools — Content Creator AI Kit
# 05: Content Calendar Generator

> AI prompts to plan 30 days of content, define your pillars, ride trends, and never stare at a blank calendar again.

---

## How to Use This File

Content calendars are only useful if they're realistic and aligned with your strategy. These prompts don't just fill slots — they build a publishing plan rooted in your goals, audience, and capacity.

**Start with Section 2 (Content Pillars)** if you haven't defined your pillars yet. Then use Section 1 to generate your calendar. Sections 3-4 are for ongoing use as trends and seasons shift.

---

## Section 1: 30-Day Content Calendar (All Platforms)

### Full Calendar Generator

```
Create a 30-day content calendar for my brand across all my platforms.

MY DETAILS:
- Niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- Target audience: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
- Platforms I'm active on: [LIST — e.g., Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, Newsletter, TikTok, Blog]
- Posting frequency per platform:
  - Twitter: [X posts per day/week]
  - LinkedIn: [X posts per week]
  - Instagram: [X posts per week — feed + stories + reels]
  - YouTube: [X videos per month — long-form + Shorts]
  - Newsletter: [X per week/month]
  - Blog: [X per month]
  - Other: [SPECIFY]

CONTENT PILLARS (the 3-5 core topics I rotate between):
1. [PILLAR 1 — e.g., "AI tools for productivity"]
2. [PILLAR 2 — e.g., "Personal brand building"]
3. [PILLAR 3 — e.g., "Behind the scenes of my business"]
4. [PILLAR 4 — e.g., "Industry news and commentary"] (optional)
5. [PILLAR 5 — e.g., "Community engagement / Q&A"] (optional)

CURRENT GOALS (pick 1-2):
- [ ] Grow followers/subscribers
- [ ] Drive traffic to [website/product/landing page]
- [ ] Build authority in my niche
- [ ] Launch a product on [DATE]
- [ ] Increase engagement (comments, replies, saves)
- [ ] Grow email list

UPCOMING EVENTS OR DATES:
- [LIST ANY RELEVANT DATES — product launches, holidays, industry events, personal milestones]

CALENDAR FORMAT:
Create a day-by-day plan for 30 days. For each day, provide:

| Day | Date | Platform(s) | Content Pillar | Content Type | Topic/Headline | Notes |
|-----|------|-------------|----------------|-------------|----------------|-------|

CONTENT TYPE OPTIONS:
- Twitter: single tweet, thread, poll, quote tweet, reply engagement
- LinkedIn: text post, carousel/document, article, poll
- Instagram: feed post, carousel, Reel, Story
- YouTube: long-form video, Shorts
- Newsletter: full edition, bonus email
- Blog: long-form post, short post, listicle

RULES:
- Rotate content pillars evenly across the month
- Vary content types within each platform (don't do 30 days of the same format)
- Include 2-3 "engagement-only" days (where I focus on replying, commenting, and community building instead of publishing)
- Schedule the highest-effort content (videos, blog posts) on days where they have buffer time
- Include repurposing notes: which content can be repurposed from which (e.g., "Repurpose Tuesday's blog post into Thursday's thread")
- Mark any posts that tie to upcoming events or launches
- Weekend content should be lighter / more personal
```

### Quick Weekly Calendar

```
Create a 7-day content plan for this week.

MY PLATFORMS: [LIST PLATFORMS]
THIS WEEK'S FOCUS: [WHAT'S THE MAIN THEME OR GOAL THIS WEEK]
CONTENT I ALREADY HAVE PLANNED: [LIST ANYTHING ALREADY SCHEDULED]
TIME AVAILABLE FOR CONTENT CREATION: [e.g., "2 hours per day" or "one 4-hour batch on Sunday"]

For each day, give me:
1. What to post and where
2. A specific topic or headline
3. Content format (tweet, thread, reel, etc.)
4. Estimated creation time
5. Repurposing opportunity (can this feed another platform?)

Keep it realistic for my time budget. Better to post 3 great things than 7 mediocre things.
```

## Section 2: Content Pillar Planning

### Pillar Discovery Prompt

```
Help me define my content pillars.

ABOUT ME:
- My niche: [YOUR NICHE]
- My expertise: [WHAT YOU KNOW DEEPLY]
- My target audience: [WHO YOU'RE CREATING FOR]
- What my audience struggles with: [THEIR TOP 3-5 PROBLEMS]
- What my audience aspires to: [THEIR TOP 3-5 GOALS]
- My content goal: [GROW AUDIENCE / SELL PRODUCTS / BUILD AUTHORITY / EDUCATE]

A CONTENT PILLAR is a broad topic category that you consistently create content around. Great pillars:
- Are broad enough to generate dozens of subtopics
- Are specific enough to attract a defined audience
- Overlap with your expertise AND your audience's needs
- Can be broken into educational, inspirational, and entertainment content

Generate 5 recommended content pillars for my brand. For each pillar:

1. PILLAR NAME: A clear, descriptive label
2. DESCRIPTION: What this pillar covers (2-3 sentences)
3. WHY THIS WORKS: Why this pillar resonates with my audience
4. SUBTOPICS: 10 specific content ideas that fall under this pillar
5. CONTENT FORMATS: Which formats work best for this pillar (threads, videos, essays, etc.)
6. POSTING RATIO: What percentage of my total content should this pillar represent

Also recommend:
- Which 3 pillars I should start with (don't try all 5 at once)
- How to balance pillars across platforms (e.g., "Pillar 3 works best on LinkedIn")
- One "wildcard" pillar that could differentiate me from competitors
```

### Subtopic Generator

```
I have these content pillars. Generate 20 specific content ideas for each one.

MY PILLARS:
1. [PILLAR 1]
2. [PILLAR 2]
3. [PILLAR 3]

For each pillar, generate 20 content ideas organized by:

EDUCATIONAL (7 ideas): Teach the audience something specific
- Format: "How to...", "Why...", "The [X]-step process for..."

INSPIRATIONAL (4 ideas): Motivate or show what's possible
- Format: Case studies, success stories, mindset shifts

CONTRARIAN (3 ideas): Challenge common beliefs in the space
- Format: "Why [popular advice] is wrong", "The truth about..."

PERSONAL (3 ideas): Behind-the-scenes, my experience, lessons learned
- Format: "What I learned from...", "My biggest mistake with..."

ENGAGEMENT (3 ideas): Questions, polls, debates designed to drive interaction
- Format: "Hot take:", "Unpopular opinion:", "Which would you rather..."

For each idea, provide:
- A working headline/title
- The best platform for this content
- Content format (tweet, thread, video, blog post, etc.)
- Difficulty to create (easy/medium/hard)
```

## Section 3: Trend-Jacking Prompt

### Current Events to Content

```
I want to create content that ties into current trends and conversations in my niche.

MY NICHE: [YOUR NICHE]
MY AUDIENCE: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
MY CONTENT PILLARS: [YOUR PILLARS]
CURRENT EVENT OR TREND: [DESCRIBE WHAT'S HAPPENING — e.g., "OpenAI just released GPT-5" or "Remote work policies are tightening at major companies" or "A viral tweet about [topic] is getting millions of impressions"]

Help me create timely content that connects this trend to my expertise.

Generate 5 content ideas:

1. HOT TAKE (Twitter/X):
A bold opinion about this trend that ties to my expertise. Include the full tweet text.

2. EDUCATIONAL ANGLE (LinkedIn or Blog):
How to help my audience understand or respond to this trend. Outline format.

3. PRACTICAL APPLICATION (Any platform):
A "how-to" that helps my audience capitalize on or adapt to this trend.

4. PERSONAL ANGLE (Instagram or Newsletter):
How this trend connects to my personal experience or my audience's daily life.

5. PREDICTIVE ANGLE (Thread or Video):
My prediction for what happens next and why my audience should care.

RULES:
- Speed matters — trend content loses value quickly. These should be publishable TODAY.
- Connect every piece back to my niche naturally. Don't force it.
- Be thoughtful, not reactive. Add genuine insight, not just commentary.
- If the trend is controversial, present a nuanced take rather than picking a side for engagement.
```

### Trend Monitoring System

```
Create a weekly trend research workflow for my content creation.

MY NICHE: [YOUR NICHE]
PLATFORMS I MONITOR: [LIST]
TIME BUDGET FOR TREND RESEARCH: [e.g., "30 minutes per day" or "1 hour on Monday"]

Build me a system that includes:

1. SOURCES TO CHECK: List the top 10 sources I should monitor weekly for trends in my niche.
   Include: Twitter accounts to follow, subreddits, newsletters, industry sites, Google Trends keywords.

2. DAILY QUICK-CHECK (5 minutes): What should I scan each morning? Give me a specific routine.

3. WEEKLY DEEP DIVE (30 minutes): A structured process for finding the week's best trend-jacking opportunities.

4. TREND EVALUATION FRAMEWORK: How do I quickly decide if a trend is worth creating content about?
   Score each trend on:
   - Relevance to my audience (1-5)
   - Timeliness — can I publish before it's old news? (1-5)
   - My unique angle — do I have something to add? (1-5)
   - Potential reach — is this trend big enough? (1-5)
   Only create content if the total score is 15+.

5. RAPID RESPONSE TEMPLATES: Give me 3 fill-in-the-blank templates I can use to publish trend content within 30 minutes of spotting an opportunity.
```

## Section 4: Seasonal Content Planning

### Quarterly Seasonal Calendar

```
Create a seasonal content plan for Q[1/2/3/4] [YEAR].

MY NICHE: [YOUR NICHE]
MY AUDIENCE: [YOUR AUDIENCE]
MY PLATFORMS: [LIST]
MY PRODUCTS/SERVICES: [ANYTHING I SELL — for tying content to offers]

For the quarter, identify:

HOLIDAYS AND OBSERVANCES:
List every relevant holiday, awareness month/week, and cultural moment in this quarter that I could create content around.

INDUSTRY EVENTS:
List major conferences, product launches, reporting seasons, or industry milestones expected this quarter.

SEASONAL THEMES:
What topics naturally come up during this season? (e.g., Q1 = new year goals, planning; Q4 = year in review, holiday buying)

For each relevant date or theme, create a content suggestion:

| Date/Period | Event/Theme | Content Idea | Platform | Content Type | Prep Time Needed |
|-------------|-------------|--------------|----------|-------------|-----------------|

PLANNING RULES:
- Mark which content needs to be prepared in advance (give lead times)
- Flag the top 5 highest-opportunity moments of the quarter
- Include "evergreen + seasonal" angles — content that ties a seasonal hook to my core topics
- Don't force connections. If a holiday isn't relevant to my niche, skip it.
- Include 1-2 "planned virality" moments — content designed to break out during peak attention periods
```

### Annual Content Themes

```
Help me plan annual content themes for my brand.

MY NICHE: [YOUR NICHE]
MY CONTENT PILLARS: [YOUR PILLARS]
MY MAJOR GOALS FOR THE YEAR:
- Q1: [GOAL]
- Q2: [GOAL]
- Q3: [GOAL]
- Q4: [GOAL]

Create a 12-month theme plan:

| Month | Monthly Theme | Why This Month | Key Content Pieces | Content Pillar Focus |
|-------|--------------|---------------|-------------------|---------------------|

THEME REQUIREMENTS:
- Each month should have a clear editorial theme that ties to a content pillar
- Themes should build on each other (create a narrative arc across the year)
- Align themes with quarterly goals (e.g., if Q2 goal is product launch, March/April themes should build anticipation)
- Include one "experimental" month where I try a new content format or platform
- Balance educational months with personal/community-building months

Also provide:
- A one-sentence content mission statement for the year
- The 3 metrics I should track to know if my content strategy is working
- One big-bet content piece per quarter (a pillar post, viral attempt, or major project)
```

## Section 5: Calendar Execution Tips

### Batch Creation Prompt

```
I have 3 hours blocked for content creation. Help me batch-create content efficiently.

MY CONTENT CALENDAR FOR THIS WEEK:
[PASTE YOUR CALENDAR OR LIST THE CONTENT YOU NEED TO CREATE]

Create a minute-by-minute schedule for my 3-hour block:

- Minute 0-10: [TASK — e.g., outline all posts]
- Minute 10-30: [TASK — e.g., write Twitter content for the week]
- ...and so on

RULES:
- Group similar tasks together (all writing, then all design, then all scheduling)
- Start with the highest-effort content while energy is high
- Include 5-minute breaks every 50 minutes
- End with scheduling everything into my publishing tool
- Include specific prompts I should use from this kit for each task

MY ENERGY LEVEL: [HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW — adjust the plan accordingly]
MY TOOLS: [LIST YOUR SCHEDULING AND CREATION TOOLS]
```

### Content Repurposing Map

```
I just created this piece of content. Map out how to repurpose it across my platforms over the next 7 days.

ORIGINAL CONTENT:
- Type: [BLOG POST / VIDEO / PODCAST / NEWSLETTER]
- Topic: [TOPIC]
- Key points covered: [LIST 3-5 KEY POINTS]
- Link: [URL — if published]

MY PLATFORMS: [LIST ALL PLATFORMS]

Create a 7-day repurposing schedule:

| Day | Platform | Content Type | What to Repurpose | Angle/Hook |
|-----|----------|-------------|-------------------|-----------|

RULES:
- Don't post the same thing everywhere on the same day
- Each repurposed piece should feel native to its platform
- Vary the angle: Day 1 might highlight Point A, Day 3 highlights Point C
- Include at least one "behind the scenes" post about creating the original
- Day 7 should be an engagement post (poll, question) related to the topic
```
