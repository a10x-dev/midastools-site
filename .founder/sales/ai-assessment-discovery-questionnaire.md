# AI Clarity Assessment — Pre-Call Brief

**Send to:** Client, 24 hours before discovery call
**Length:** ~10 minutes to complete
**Purpose:** Prime the conversation, not replace it. The real insights come from the live follow-up questions.

## Optional bonus questions (nice-to-have)

> *Skip if short on time — I&apos;ll ask these on the call anyway.*

- **What&apos;s the next $200K of revenue going to come from?** (helps me prioritize tools that move that needle)
- **What&apos;s your current tool stack?** (CRM, email, calendar, accounting, project mgmt — name them)
- **Who&apos;s on your team, full-time or part-time?** (head count, roles)
- **Have you read or implemented any AI &quot;system&quot; in the last 6 months?** (newsletters, courses, frameworks — names them)

## Internal use — analyst checklist before the call

After the client returns this brief and BEFORE the call, the analyst:

- [ ] Re-read all 8 answers
- [ ] Visit the client&apos;s website + LinkedIn + 1 piece of their content
- [ ] Check if they&apos;re an existing subscriber (`.founder/data/subscribers/`) and pull any prior intel
- [ ] Note 3 follow-up questions to ask live (do NOT pre-write the report)
- [ ] Prepare 2-3 candidate tools to mention (without committing) for live calibration
- [ ] Confirm Zoom link works, recording is enabled, scheduler shows correct time zone
