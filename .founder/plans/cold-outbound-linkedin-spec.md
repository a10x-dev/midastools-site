## 1. Why LinkedIn (not X, not email scraping, not Facebook groups)

| Channel | Audience match | Compliance | Cost | Reply rate baseline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn DM (warm via connection) | ★★★★ | ✅ Native channel | $0 (manual) | 8-15% |
| LinkedIn DM (Sales Navigator + InMail) | ★★★★ | ✅ Native channel | $99/mo | 4-8% |
| Cold email (scraped) | ★★★ | ⚠️ CAN-SPAM gray | $0-$200 enrichment | 1-3% |
| X/Twitter DM | ★★ | ✅ but spammy | $0 | <1% |
| Facebook coach groups | ★★ | ⚠️ posting policy varies | $0 | <2% |

**Pick:** LinkedIn DM via free account, manual personalization, target audience that already publishes content publicly. We trade volume for quality; we already know volume-content doesn't move our KPIs.

## 2. ICP definition — who we're targeting

### Hard filters (must match all)
- Title: Coach, Consultant, Advisor, Founder (solo), Strategist, Operator
- Audience: 1,000-50,000 LinkedIn followers (big enough to have revenue, small enough to be reachable)
- Posts about AI/ChatGPT at least 1× in last 60 days (proves topic interest)
- Solo or 1-2 person team (LinkedIn shows "1-10 employees" + no team listed on About)
- Has a paid offer (course, coaching, consulting) visible on profile or website
- Geography: US, Canada, UK, Australia, Western Europe, NZ (English + USD/EUR/GBP wallets)

### Soft signals (boost score)
- Has posted "I tried ChatGPT for X" or "AI didn't work for my biz" — pain signal
- Has shared a prompt screenshot — already prompt-curious
- Sells coaching at $1K-$10K per package — wallet size matches $297-997 SKU
- Newsletter or Substack with 1K+ subs — distribution-capable, can refer

### Hard rejects
- ❌ AI consultants themselves (we can't sell them an AI audit)
- ❌ "Founder" of 50+ employee company (not solo)
- ❌ Pure-services agency owners (different sales motion, different price ceiling)
- ❌ MLM/coaching-the-coaches (bad audience hygiene)
- ❌ <500 followers (no audience = no urgency to use AI well)

## 5. Message framework — the 3-touch sequence

### Touch 1 — DM Day 0 (under 90 words)

> Hey [first name] — saw your post about [specific recent post]. Loved the part about [specific quote, paraphrased].
>
> I run a small research desk that does AI audits for solo coaches/consultants. Most folks I talk to are using ChatGPT for the wrong 30% of their work and missing the 70% where it actually compounds.
>
> Not selling — would just love to compare notes. If you're up for a 15-min trade, I'll share the 14-question audit I run on every client; you tell me what you've actually tried that worked. Worst case: you keep the framework.
>
> — [Name], midastools.co

### Touch 2 — DM Day 5 (under 60 words, if no reply)

> Hey [first name] — quick follow-up: I just put together a [specific deliverable matching their niche, e.g., "5-prompt sermon-prep workflow" / "MLS rewrite prompt for realtors"]. Want me to send it? One word reply: "send."
>
> No pitch attached. Just a free thing because I dug into your work.

### Touch 3 — DM Day 12 (the breakup, under 30 words)

> [first name] — closing the loop. If audit-talk isn't useful right now, no worries. I'll bow out. If timing changes, I'm at iam@armando.mx.

### Why this works
- Touch 1: specificity (their content) + low-ask (15-min trade) + giveaway (the framework) + identity-anchor (research desk, not salesperson)
- Touch 2: free deliverable matched to THEIR niche (not generic) + one-word reply lowers friction
- Touch 3: reverse-psychology breakup; reply rates spike 5-15% on this one

## 6. Conversion math — 1-month target

| Stage | Funnel rate | N |
|---|---|---|
| DMs sent (1 touch each = 3 DMs per prospect) | 100% | 50 prospects → 150 DMs across 28 days |
| Replies (any) | 12% | 6 prospects |
| Audit calls booked | 33% of repliers | 2 calls |
| Calls → $297 mini-audit OR $997 close | 50% of calls | **1 paid sale** |

**1 paid sale per 50 prospects = $297-997 per ~150 DMs sent.**

That's the floor. If reply rate is 8% (conservative) we still get 4 prospects engaged → 1 buy ≈ $497 expected revenue per 50-prospect batch.

**Time cost:** 50 prospects × 5 min research + 5 min message = ~8 hrs over the month. **$60-120/hr blended at the lower end.** Beats every channel we've tested.

## 7. Compliance + LinkedIn hygiene

- ✅ Free LinkedIn account: limit ~20 connection-requests + ~30 DMs per day to avoid restrictions
- ✅ Personalize every DM (LinkedIn ML penalizes templates)
- ✅ Don't follow up more than 2× (Touch 2 + Touch 3 only)
- ✅ Always include unsubscribe-equivalent ("if not useful, no worries")
- ❌ Never use scraping tools that violate LinkedIn ToS (Phantombuster, Dux-Soup, etc.) before we have product-market-fit signal
- ❌ Never paste a Stripe link in Touch 1 or Touch 2
