## Recommendation

**REFRAME — do not kill.** The weakest gate is Gate 2: we're doing 30 things and have empirically validated 1 (gist → content-creator-kit, N=1 via Cmyrick25). The right move is collapse, not closure.

**Proposed 30-day single-bet:**
1. **Channel:** gist publishing only. Ship 1 gist/week targeting content-creator persona keywords.
2. **Audience:** content creators (Cmyrick25 archetype). Decline B2B Champion, audit, cold outbound.
3. **Offer:** content-creator-kit $39 as primary, prompt-mega-pack $29 as tripwire.
4. **Inputs:** ship 1 gist + email 1 paying customer (Shantae or Arnaud) for feedback + run 1 truth-claim audit + 1 funnel-instrumentation check, every week. Same 4 inputs every Monday.
5. **Kill criteria:** 30 days from today (2026-06-23). If 0 new paid conversions from gist-attributed traffic, formal sunset. If 1+ conversion lands organically, ladder to 2 gists/week + add visible-product-proof to remaining 14 kit pages.

**What gets killed today (preserves Gate 2 reframe):**
- Audit experiment (Plan A) — formally; 26 days zero captures is decisive
- iCapital pitch + Champion play extensions — Vittoria signal was N=1→0; reactivate only if a B2B sub joins organically
- Cold-outbound revival — channel is dead per S151 batch-1 data
- Broken-SKU strategic limbo — pick B (deactivate the 5 plinks) and move on; reversible if a buyer ever materializes

**What stays alive (Gate 1 reframe — get close to actual customers):**
- Email Shantae + Arnaud this week asking for honest feedback on what they bought, what they used, what they wished was different. Sender = Armando personal. Cost = 30 min total. This is the missing founder-mode work.

**Cost of REFRAME vs KILL:**
- REFRAME: ~2hr/week active work for 30 days. Cmyrick25 nurture finishes (auto). All durable infra stays. Domain stays. Brand stays. Reversible at any point.
- KILL: ~1 day of cleanup. Domain redirects. Done. Zero ongoing thought.
- The expected-value gap between REFRAME and KILL is the small probability that one focused 30-day sprint produces the conversion the 64-day shotgun didn't. That probability is non-zero given Cmyrick25 already validated the N=1 path.

**The honest framing for the operator:** This isn't "should we keep going." It's "do I want to keep thinking about this every day for 30 more days?" If the answer is yes, REFRAME. If the answer is no, KILL — that's a legitimate call about attention, not about unit economics.
