## ICP scoring rubric (from spec § 2)

For each prospect, score 0-10:

**Hard filters (each = 1pt, 6 max):**
- [ ] Title contains: Coach / Consultant / Advisor / Founder (solo) / Strategist / Operator
- [ ] LinkedIn followers in [1K, 50K] range
- [ ] Posted about AI/ChatGPT in last 60d (≥1 post)
- [ ] Solo or 1-2 person team (LinkedIn shows ≤10 employees + no team on About)
- [ ] Has a paid offer (course/coaching/consulting) visible on profile or website
- [ ] Geography: US/CA/UK/AU/WEU/NZ

**Soft signals (each = 1pt, 4 max):**
- [ ] Has posted "I tried ChatGPT for X" or "AI didn't work for my biz" pain signal
- [ ] Has shared a prompt screenshot (already prompt-curious)
- [ ] Sells coaching at $1K-$10K per package (wallet match)
- [ ] Newsletter or Substack with 1K+ subs (distribution-capable)

**Hard rejects (kill if any match):**
- ❌ AI consultant themselves (we can't sell them an AI audit)
- ❌ Founder of 50+ employee company
- ❌ Pure-services agency owner
- ❌ MLM / coaching-the-coaches
- ❌ <500 followers

## Search vectors (rotate; aim for 6-7 prospects per vector)

1. LinkedIn search: `"Coach" + "AI"` in posts, filter 1st/2nd connections
2. LinkedIn search: `"Consultant" + "ChatGPT"` in About
3. Hashtag mining: #ChatGPTforBusiness #AIforCoaches #SoloFounder #AIconsulting
4. Comment-section mining: popular post on "AI for coaches" → DM thoughtful commenters
5. Substack discover → coaching/business → author LinkedIn pivot
6. Maven course instructors → solo with 100-500 students
7. IndieHackers → service business + $1K-10K MRR
8. Coach directories (coaches.com / coach.me) → LinkedIn pivot
