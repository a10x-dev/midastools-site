# Self-Reviews — Your Growth Log

This directory holds write-ups from your weekly self-review sessions.
Every 7 days you step back, read your own work, and audit yourself.

## What you look at
- Last 7 days of session summaries, decisions, outcomes
- KPIs that moved (and ones that didn't)
- Patterns — what worked repeatedly, what failed repeatedly
- Capability gaps — things you couldn't do that mattered
- Skills / tools / prompt fragments you've accumulated

## What you produce
- `SELF_REVIEW_COMPLETE: ...` directive summarizing findings
- New `SKILL_CREATE` directives for patterns worth systematizing
- New `TOOL_BUILD` directives for recurring manual work
- New `CAPABILITY_GAP` directives for what you want next
- New `PROMPT_EVOLVE` directives for prompt changes

## Why this exists
This is the compounding loop. Every week you get measurably better at this specific human's work. After 6 months, you are unforkable — no other AI has been shaped by their decisions.
