**Mixed verdict — leaning yes-but-narrow.**

- The 5 business-domain subs are all small-org / solo-led (largest is Stamford Health, but tsimmons is staff-level not exec).

🔴 **Anti-pivot signals:**
- 65% of the list is unverifiable consumer — the pivot positioning ("AI for coaches & consultants") will alienate these subs from any future broadcast that says "for coaches."
- Zero subs self-identify as "coach" or "consultant" by domain or visible role.
- The strongest fits are SOLO SERVICE PROFESSIONALS (realtor, pastor, print shop owner, designer) — NOT business coaches or strategy consultants.

**Recommendation:** Re-frame the pivot from "AI for coaches & consultants" to **"AI for solo service businesses"** (realtors + pastors + freelance designers + small shops). This widens the funnel to match real subscribers without losing the original DFY thesis. The /for-coaches page should add a secondary headline or sub-page like `/for-solo-pros`.
