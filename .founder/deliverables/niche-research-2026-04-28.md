## Section 3 — Recommended Single Niche + Positioning

### Pick: **Solo Coaches & Consultants**

**Justification:**

1. **Domain name fit ("midastools" → wealth/money/Midas touch):**
   The dominant pain in coaching is monetization. Coaches are obsessed with "turning my expertise into income," "scaling without burning out," "getting paid what I'm worth." Midas = wealth alchemy. Tagline candidate: **"Midas Tools — turn your coaching practice into a money machine."** The Midas frame literally sells better here than to any other niche on the list. Real estate agents care about deals; plumbers care about jobs; coaches care about *transformation into revenue*.

2. **Existing assets map cleanly:**
   - Brand Starter Pack DFY ($149) → coach branding package. Direct fit.
   - AI Content Month DFY ($299) → coach content engine. Direct fit. Coaches need 30 IG/blog/email pieces a month. This is the #1 thing they pay agencies for.
   - 21 prompt kits → re-frame the top 6 (content, branding, sales pages, email, Instagram, lead magnets) as "Coach Editions."
   - Listing Optimizer keeps a side life as a real-estate-agent product (sub-vertical, secondary).

3. **Mainstream non-technical persona match:**
   Our subscriber data shows 48% Yahoo+AOL emails (age 45+, mainstream). Coach demographic is exactly this — second-career women 35–60 who are AI-curious but not technical. Our "personal-brand consultant" subscriber is essentially already a coach. The German print/media subscriber, the church/ministry subscriber, the personal-brand consultant — ALL of these can be re-served as coaches/consultants with the same kit catalog.

4. **Wallet validation:**
   $7.3B coaching market, $99 prompt playbooks already proven (Imagine Social Media), $1,500–$5,000 DFY brand packages already sold by coach-specialist agencies. They pay because their clients pay them $200–$500/hour — high margin, content-bottlenecked profession.

5. **No dominant brand owns "AI for Coaches":**
   Paperbell owns CRM; Thinkific owns courses; CoachVox owns AI clones. NO ONE owns "the AI productivity layer for coaches." That is the seat we take.

### Positioning one-liner

> **"Midas Tools — the AI productivity stack that turns coaching expertise into recurring revenue. Done-for-you content, branding, and lead-gen kits built for solo coaches and consultants."**

Sub-headline: **"Stop trading hours for dollars. Let AI run your content, brand, and pipeline while you coach."**

## TL;DR
**Pivot to solo coaches & consultants.** Largest cash-flush mainstream-consumer niche, perfect Midas-touch domain alignment, existing kits + DFY services map 1:1, no dominant AI-for-coaches brand owns the seat, and our subscriber data already skews coach-adjacent.
