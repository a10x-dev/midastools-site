## Why "Coaches & Consultants" beats the alternatives

Built from two parallel research threads (`niche-research-2026-04-28.md` + `yc-rfs-persona-rescored-2026-04-28.md`). They converged independently.

### The 4 dimensions that filtered everything

| Dimension | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| **Mainstream non-technical persona** | Subscriber data: 48% Yahoo+AOL, age 45+. Confirms NOT a developer audience. Kills MCP / agentic / F100 plays. |
| **Domain name "midastools" (Midas touch = wealth alchemy)** | Niche must care about *turning expertise into money*. Coaches are obsessed with this. Plumbers care about jobs, not transformation. |
| **Existing assets (21 prompt kits + 3 DFY services)** | $299 Content Month + $149 Brand Pack = literally what coaches already buy from agencies for $1,500–$5,000. 1:1 fit. |
| **Open lane (no dominant AI brand owns the niche)** | Paperbell owns CRM. Thinkific owns courses. CoachVox owns AI clones. **No one owns "AI productivity for coaches."** That's our seat. |

### Niche scorecard (top 3)

| Niche | Market | Wallet | Competition | Channel match | Asset fit | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Coaches/Consultants** | $7.3B US, ~88K coaching businesses | 🟢 $99 prompt playbooks proven; $1,500–$5K DFY brand packs proven | 🟢 Open lane (no dominant AI-prompt brand) | 🟢 FB groups, IG Reels, Pinterest, podcasts — already where our content lives | 🟢 1:1 fit | **PIVOT** |
| Real Estate | 1.5M agents, 24% spend $500+/mo on tech | 🟢 Highest wallet | 🔴 Red ocean (Lofty, Lone Wolf, Writor, Write.Homes, ListingAI...) | 🔴 NAR-walled, brokerage Slacks | 🟡 Listing Optimizer fits | **Plan B sub-vertical** |
| Solopreneurs (current ICP) | 41.8M US | 🔴 $530/mo avg, $20/mo AI budget | 🔴 Brutal (God of Prompt, every Gumroad seller) | 🟢 We can reach them | 🟢 Fits today | **THIS IS WHY WE'RE AT $0 — MOVE ON** |

Etsy, local services, legal solos all ruled out (see niche-research memo for detail).

### The "current ICP doesn't pay" insight

Our existing 21-kit catalog targets the solopreneur/side-hustler — **41.8M US potential customers, $530/mo average earnings, $20/mo AI budget**. Mass-market pricing pressure + saturated competition (God of Prompt is a $150 bundle with 30K prompts) = $0 revenue is the predictable outcome. We don't need a better funnel; we need a better customer.

A coach billing clients $200/hour treats $97 as a no-brainer. A side-hustler making $530/month treats it as a serious purchase decision. **Same product, 10x conversion just from picking the right wallet.**

### Brand & positioning (Week 1)
- **Homepage hero rewrite**: from "21 AI Prompt Kits" → "Turn Your Expertise Into Recurring Revenue. AI tools and DFY services built for coaches, consultants, and experts."

- **Top 6 prompt kits relabeled "Coach Edition"** — same files, new covers + landing copy (Content Creator Kit → "Coach Content Engine"; Email Marketing Kit → "Coach Email Pipeline"; SaaS Founder Kit → "Solo Consultant Kit").

### Product additions (Week 1–2)
- **`/for-coaches` landing page** — single most important new asset. Hero, 3-tier pricing (kits → DFY → custom retainer), FAQ, social proof.
- **2 lead magnets**:
  - "Coach Content Calendar — 90 Days" (PDF + Notion)
  - "Coach Niche Finder Worksheet"
- **Reframe `/starter-pack` $9 tripwire** → "Coach Starter Pack: 20 prompts that fill your calendar."

### Content engine (Week 2)

Each: 1,500+ words, FAQ schema, ends with email gate + tripwire CTA.

### Distribution (Week 3)
- **Facebook groups (top channel for this niche):** join 10 largest coach groups, give value 5 days, soft mention with lead magnet. Per-group rules.
- **Coach podcasts:** cold-pitch 20 niche coach podcasts. Hook: "How coaches use AI to 10x content output." Free Coach Content Calendar as bait.
- **Pinterest:** 30 pins → coach lead magnets (Pinterest dominates coach discovery for women 35+).
- **Cold email via Vibe Prospecting:** 50 coaches/week, pitch DFY AI Content Month as "your Q3 content engine."
- **Affiliate seeding:** 30% rev-share offer to 10 mid-tier coach influencers.

- ✅ **Keep** $9 tripwire / free tools / email gate — funnel architecture is sound.
- ✅ **Keep** Listing Optimizer DFY — relabeled "Coach Sales Page Optimizer" (primary) with real-estate-agent landing as Plan B sub-vertical.
- 🔪 **Kill** the "AI for everyone" homepage messaging. Niching narrows; that's the point.
- 🔪 **Kill** any plan to ship MCP / agent integration in the next 30 days. Wrong audience.
- 🔪 **Kill** the F100 / RFS #13 enterprise pitch as core strategy. Use RFS #2 wording in coach-DFY follow-ups instead.
- ⏸️ **Defer** YC S26 application. Apply S27 only after $5K MRR.

## Why NOT just do the GSC title fixes & ship gist #14?

Those are productivity moves, not strategy moves. Two zero-CTR pages getting 850 imp/mo can be fixed in 15 min — **and we should still do it tonight.** But they don't change the underlying problem: even if we 10x the SERP click yield, we'd be selling $29 prompt packs to a $530/mo audience and remain at $0 revenue. The GSC fix is tactical; the niche pivot is strategic. Do both.
