## Cited Benchmarks

### Cold-pitch reply rates (small → larger creator)
- B2B cold email average: **3-5.1% reply**, top quartile 15-25%, top decile 8-12%. Source: [Built For B2B 10K-campaign study](https://www.builtforb2b.com/blog/b2b-cold-email-benchmark-2025)
- Hyper-targeted small-list cold (≤50 prospects): **5.8% reply**. Source: [Belkins 2025 via Built For B2B](https://www.builtforb2b.com/blog/b2b-cold-email-benchmark-2025)
- Podcast/creator pitch (warmer analog, topic-matched): **10-23% reply**, PodPitch 500K-pitch avg = 23%. Source: [Respona](https://respona.com/blog/podcast-outreach/), [PodPitch](https://www.podpitch.com/)

### Newsletter mention CTR
- SparkLoop magic-link CTR: **~4%** of recipients (1,100-sub case study). Source: [aazarshad.com](https://aazarshad.com/resources/sparkloop-review/)
- beehiiv Boost open rate: **>40%**, single embedded recommendation CTR: **0.3-2%** of audience. Source: [beehiiv Boosts](https://www.beehiiv.com/features/boosts)
- Newsletter swap conversion: **3-5% when audience match is strong**, 50-500 net subs per swap depending on size. Source: [beehiiv swap examples](https://www.beehiiv.com/blog/newsletter-swap-examples), [Indiegraf](https://indiegraf.com/blog/indie-publisher/newsletter-swap/)

### Click-to-signup conversion
- Newsletter signup pages: **10-20% conversion** (Q4 2024 average). Source: [Unbounce 2024](https://unbounce.com/average-conversion-rates-landing-pages/)
- Well-designed lead magnets: **30-50%** when offer is narrow and immediately useful. Source: [Focus Digital](https://focus-digital.co/lead-magnet-conversion-rate-by-industry/)
- Quiz/assessment magnets: 40%+; templates/checklists: 15-25%. Source: [Amra & Elma](https://www.amraandelma.com/lead-magnet-conversion-statistics/)

### Free → paid (digital info-product)
- Lead-to-sale: **0.6-1.6%** across industries. Source: [Focus Digital](https://focus-digital.co/lead-magnet-conversion-rate-by-industry/)
- SaaS template-magnet with systematic follow-up: **~5%** free→paid. Source: same
- Companies with nurture sequences: **25-35% above benchmark**. Source: same

### 2. Beehiiv Boost as paid alternative (NEW OPTION)
AI Finance Club is hosted on beehiiv. beehiiv Boost network connects creators for paid recommendations: pay $1.63-$2.37 per qualified subscriber acquired, with 40%+ open rate. Source: [SparkLoop guide](https://help.sparkloop.app/en/articles/9763305-4-step-guide-to-maximizing-your-sparkloop-earnings).

Unit economics with our funnel:
- 100 subs at $2 CPA = $200 spend
- 100 subs × 30% nurture-engaged × 2% upgrade = **0.6 sales = $58 revenue**
- **Negative ROI on $97 SKU at 2% upgrade rate.**
- Break-even requires upgrade rate ≥ **3.4%** (CPA $2 / $97 = 2.06%, plus margin)

**Implication:** Boost is NOT a clean win at our current $97 SKU upgrade rate. It only becomes attractive if (a) we ladder to higher-LTV recurring offer, OR (b) free claim → average revenue per visitor exceeds $2.40.
