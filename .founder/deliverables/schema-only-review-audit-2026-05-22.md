## Risk basis

FTC 16 CFR Part 255 (final rule, August 2024) prohibits fake / AI-generated / placeholder consumer reviews. JSON-LD-only reviews that Google sees but the human visitor cannot see fail the disclosure standard AND fail the substantiation standard (no verifiable customer trail). Even if the quotes happen to be real, their schema-only placement presents them to search engines without rendering them to the buyer — this is the exact pattern the rule targets.

## Remediation options

1. **Remove the `review` array + `aggregateRating` block** from JSON-LD on every flagged page (smallest, reversible, FTC-safe).

2. **Render the same testimonials in page body** with author attribution + date + verification context (only if quotes are real and you can cite the source).

3. **Replace with real customer quotes** from Vittoria / Shantae / Arnaud / George if they consent (4 LTM buyers).
