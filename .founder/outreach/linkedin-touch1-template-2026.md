# LinkedIn Touch-1 DM — Master Templates

**Spec:** `.founder/plans/cold-outbound-linkedin-spec.md` § 5
**Use:** First DM in the 3-touch sequence. Copy a variant matching prospect's vertical, fill bracketed slots, send via LinkedIn web (manual to comply with LinkedIn ToS — no automation).

**Hard rules per spec:**
- Under 90 words
- ZERO Stripe link in Touch 1
- Reference a SPECIFIC recent post or piece of their content (not generic flattery)
- Identity-anchor as research desk, not salesperson
- Always include a low-ask exit ramp
