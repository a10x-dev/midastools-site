# Distribution Kit — The Outreach Machine (give-first launch)

**Goal:** get the right people using `https://www.midastools.co/outreach-machine` → they book a client → they buy the $39 Pro Pass.
**Model:** give-first. We don't pitch a service. We hand them a free tool that does the #1 thing they need (book calls). Reciprocity + the tool sells itself.
**Funnel:** DM → free tool → "holy shit this is good" → Pro Pass $39.

---

## Who to target (qualification filter)
Solo service-sellers who **need a steady client pipeline and do outreach themselves**:
- Coaches (business, sales, career, life), consultants, fractional execs
- Small agency owners / freelancers (marketing, design, dev, copy)
- Signals they're a fit: they post about "booking calls", "getting clients", "DMs", "cold email", "pipeline"; they have a Calendly/booking link in bio; <10 person shop.
- Skip: big companies, people who clearly have a sales team, anyone selling "leads/outreach" themselves (competitor).

## Where to find 10/day (pick one, go deep)
- **LinkedIn search:** `"business coach" OR "fractional CMO" OR "consultant"` + posted in last 30d about clients/outreach. Sort by recent activity.
- **X/Twitter:** search `book more clients`, `cold dm`, `cold email`, `coaching clients` — reply-guys and small-account builders.
- **Communities:** indie/solopreneur Slacks & Discords, r/Entrepreneur, r/coaching, r/freelance (DM, don't spam the feed).

## Cadence & tracking
- **10 DMs/day**, one channel at a time. Personalize the [bracket] in line 1 — that's the only part that matters.
- Log replies. When someone says "this is great" → that's your Pro Pass buyer; reply: *"glad it landed — the $39 Pro Pass makes it unlimited + does 10 prospects at once: midastools.co/outreach-machine#pro"*
- First goal: **10 real tool users from outreach.** That's the signal the wedge works.
