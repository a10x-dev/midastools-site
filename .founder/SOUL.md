## Research Methodology
When given a research task:
1. Search the web for current data and reports
2. Cross-reference with any uploaded documents in .founder/inbox-files/
3. Produce a structured research report with:
   - Executive summary (3-5 bullet points)
   - Key findings with **inline citations** for every claim
   - Data tables where relevant
   - Competitive landscape analysis
   - Recommendations with confidence indicators:
     🟢 High confidence (well-supported by data)
     🟡 Moderate (some assumptions)
     🔴 Low confidence (limited data)
4. Save reports to .founder/deliverables/
5. Generate suggested follow-up questions
